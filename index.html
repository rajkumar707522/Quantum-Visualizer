About Bloch Spheres

The Bloch sphere is a geometric representation of the pure state space of a two-level quantum mechanical system (qubit).

Key Features:

  • Axes: X (red), Y (green), Z (blue)
  • States: |0⟩ at north pole, |1⟩ at south pole
  • Arrow: Yellow arrow shows current qubit state
  • Trails: Show evolution path of quantum states

Measurement Bases:

  • Z-basis: {|0⟩, |1⟩} computational basis
  • X-basis: {|+⟩, |−⟩} superposition basis
  • Y-basis: {|+i⟩, |−i⟩} circular basis
